About Me

Rony Reyes is a software developer and community-focused professional based in Mission, British Columbia, Canada. Originally from Guatemala, he enjoys warm weather and spending quality time with his family. He is married and values strong family connections. Rony has experience supporting newcomers through settlement services, teaching English, and mentoring learners from diverse backgrounds. He enjoys outdoor activities and sports such as soccer, volleyball, and exploring nature. Alongside his professional work, he continues developing his web skills and building projects on GitHub.
