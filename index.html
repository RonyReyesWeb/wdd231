About Me

Rony Reyes is a software developer and community-focused professional based in Mission, British Columbia, Canada. Originally from Guatemala, he has experience supporting newcomers through settlement services and educational mentoring, and has worked with diverse communities, including teaching English to newcomers. Married and family-oriented, Rony enjoys warm weather, spending time with his loved ones, and playing sports such as surfing, soccer, and volleyball. He is also actively learning web technologies, building projects on GitHub, and sharing knowledge with learners through mentorship and volunteer initiatives.
